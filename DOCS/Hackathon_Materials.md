# Hackathon Presentation: Multilingual Code-Mixed In-Car AI Assistant

## 1. Problem Statement
In India, communication is rarely monolingual. Over 500 million people speak "Hinglish" or other code-mixed languages. Standard car assistants (Alexa/Siri) often fail to understand natural commands like *"AC thoda kam karo"* or *"Sunroof band kar do please"*. This creates a barrier for safe, hands-free car operation for a majority of the population.

## 2. Why Code-Mixed AI is Important
- **Linguistic Reality:** Code-mixing is the natural way of speaking for billions in the Global South.
- **Safety:** Natural language understanding reduces cognitive load while driving.
- **Accessibility:** Lowers the barrier for non-native English speakers to use advanced car features.

## 4. Business Potential
- **B2B (OEMs):** Licensing the NLP engine to Tata Motors, Mahindra, etc.
- **Data Analytics:** Insights into driver comfort preferences for personalized insurance (UBI).
- **Aftermarket:** Smart HUD/Dongle for older cars using this AI frontend.

## 5. Future Scope
- **IoT Integration:** Connecting with smart home systems ("I'm 10 mins away, geyser on kar do").
- **Offline Mode:** Using TinyML for edge processing of critical commands (Brakes/Emergency).
- **Emotion Engine:** Detecting driver fatigue or stress via voice tone and adjusting cabin environment.
